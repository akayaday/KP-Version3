For MIT's Externship Program, I was paired for a January internship at Ahalife, a company founded by MIT Alumni Shauna Mei and Matthew Wilkerson. Ahalife is an curated marketplace for uniquely crafted goods and luxury items. I came on during the month of January, and while I also worked on the user interface for the main parent site, Ahalife, I came to help design and complete the user experience for Ahanoir, the new marketplace specifically focused towards sexual products and experiences, as well as intrguing articles. This microsite promotes sexual health and helps to encourage discovery and tasteful indulgenec. While a very unique demographic to create for, Ahanoir released February 4th, 2014.
